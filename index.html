When Health Outpaces Wealth

A Data-Driven Analysis of Global Longevity and Economic Prosperity (1950-2022).

View Scatter Plot View Countries Heat Map

Project Description

This analysis examines the evolution of global life expectancy and economic prosperity from 1950 to 2022. By visualizing the relationship between life expectancy at birth and GDP per capita, we uncover trends, disparities, and insights into how health outcomes have evolved in relation to economic growth across different countries and regions.

Data Source

The dataset, aggregated by Our World in Data (OWID), combines national health and economic metrics from 1543 to the present day.
